Trouble Shooting

Heroku 'Permission denied (publickey) fatal: Could not read from remote repository'

ssh-keygen -t rsa
heroku keys:add

or just

ssh-add ~/.ssh/id_rsa
#and, to confirm it's been added to the known list of keys
ssh-add -l
